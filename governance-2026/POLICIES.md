# DGCP Governance 2026 — Policies

**Framework:** DGCP™ (Data Governance & Continuous Proof)  
**Organization:** MaMeeFarm™  
**Status:** Active / Time-Bound Policies  
**Temporal Scope:** Governance Year 2026

---

## Purpose

This document defines **operational governance policies** applicable during the 2026 governance year.

These policies implement DGCP™ core principles in real-world operation, providing clear, auditable rules without redefining constitutional governance.

---

## Policy Principles

All policies listed herein adhere to the following principles:

- **Time-bound applicability** — valid only within 2026 unless renewed
- **Non-destructive enforcement** — no erasure of historical records
- **Append-only governance** — policies may be supplemented, not overwritten
- **Human accountability** — enforcement decisions remain human-authoritative
- **Audit readiness** — suitable for institutional and regulatory review

---

## Policy Registry

### POL-2026-001 — Platform Non-Reliance (Operational)

**Status:** Active  
**Effective Date:** 2026-01-01  
**Scope:** All DGCP™ operations and disclosures

**Policy Statement:**  
DGCP™ operations in 2026 shall not rely on any single platform for authority, validity, or persistence of governance records.

**Operational Rules:**  
- Social media platforms are narrative-only  
- Git repositories, cryptographic timestamps, and verifiable records remain authoritative  
- Platform availability does not determine governance validity

**Reference:**  
DGCP™ Core Governance — Platform Non-Reliance

---

### POL-2026-002 — Evidence Integrity and Time Authority

**Status:** Active  
**Effective Date:** 2026-01-01  
**Scope:** All proofs, records, and governance actions

**Policy Statement:**  
All DGCP™ evidence and governance actions must maintain cryptographic integrity and explicit time authority.

**Operational Rules:**  
- All material records require verifiable timestamps  
- Time authority must be explicit and auditable  
- Narrative timing does not supersede cryptographic time

**Reference:**  
DGCP™ Core Governance — Time Authority Hierarchy

---

### POL-2026-003 — AI Non-Training Enforcement

**Status:** Active  
**Effective Date:** 2026-01-01  
**Scope:** All data access and external usage

**Policy Statement:**  
DGCP™ data shall not be used for training, fine-tuning, or benchmarking of AI or machine learning models without explicit written authorization.

**Operational Rules:**  
- AI training is prohibited by default  
- Any exception requires documented approval and licensing  
- Unauthorized AI usage triggers governance review

**Reference:**  
DGCP™ Core Governance — AI Boundaries

---

### POL-2026-004 — Privacy Preservation and Safety-by-Design

**Status:** Active  
**Effective Date:** 2026-01-01  
**Scope:** Data handling, disclosure, and publication

**Policy Statement:**  
Privacy preservation and contributor safety are mandatory governance requirements.

**Operational Rules:**  
- Data minimization is enforced  
- Sensitive information is not publicly disclosed  
- Safety considerations precede publication decisions

**Reference:**  
DGCP™ Core Governance — Data Safety and Human-First Principles

---

### POL-2026-005 — Governance Change Control

**Status:** Active  
**Effective Date:** 2026-01-01  
**Scope:** Governance modifications and updates

**Policy Statement:**  
All governance changes in 2026 must follow formal documentation and review procedures.

**Operational Rules:**  
- Changes are recorded in `CHANGELOG.md`  
- Decisions are recorded in `DECISIONS.md`  
- No retroactive modification of records is permitted

**Reference:**  
DGCP™ Governance 2026 — Change Management Principles
